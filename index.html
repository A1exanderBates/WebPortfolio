About Me

Hi! I'm Alexander and I'm 21 years old. I just graduated with a B.A. in Public Health Studies from Johns Hopkins University.

I was born with profound bilateral sensorineural hearing loss, and I received Cochlear Implants at a young age. I've always tried to make sense of the world visually, which is why I'm passionate about using data science to explore public health.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, podcasts, running, and recently, front-end programming.
